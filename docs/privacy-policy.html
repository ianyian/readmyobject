1. Introduction

Welcome to Poker Chip Counter ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS application. Your privacy is extremely important to us.

The short version: We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. All processing happens locally on your device.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect ZERO personal information.

  • No names
  • No email addresses
  • No phone numbers
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers
  • No IP addresses

2.2 Photos and Camera Access

The App requires access to your camera and photo library to perform object detection. All image processing happens entirely on your device.

  • Photos are NEVER uploaded to any server
  • Photos are NEVER stored by the App (unless you explicitly save them using iOS system functions)
  • Photos are NEVER shared with us or third parties
  • The App only analyzes the photos you choose in real-time
  • When you close the App, all processing data is discarded

2.3 Usage Data and Analytics

We collect ZERO analytics or usage data.

  • No tracking of how you use the App
  • No crash reports sent to us
  • No feature usage statistics
  • No performance metrics sent externally

3. How We Use Your Information

Since we don't collect any information, we don't use any information. All image processing and object detection happens:

  • Locally - On your iPhone/iPad only
  • Privately - No data leaves your device
  • Instantly - Real-time processing with no delays
  • Offline - No internet connection required

4. Data Storage

4.1 On Your Device

The App stores only the following preferences locally on your device:

  • Your appearance preference (Light/Dark/System mode)
  • Your confidence threshold setting (10-95%)
  • Your last selected detection mode

These preferences never leave your device and can be reset by deleting the App.

4.2 On Our Servers

We don't have any servers. We don't operate any backend infrastructure. There is nowhere for your data to be sent even if we wanted to.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We share NOTHING with third parties because we have nothing to share.

  • No advertising networks
  • No analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • No social media integrations
  • No affiliate tracking
  • No data brokers

7. Children's Privacy

The App is safe for users of all ages. We don't collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The App is fully compliant with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).

8. International Users

The App works identically for users worldwide. Since all processing happens on your device, there are no international data transfer concerns. The App is fully compliant with:

  • GDPR (European Union)

9. Security

The best security is not collecting data in the first place. Since the App processes everything locally on your device and doesn't transmit any data:

  • There's no risk of data breaches
  • There's no risk of unauthorized access to servers
  • Your data is as secure as your iPhone/iPad
  • We recommend using iOS security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID)

10. Machine Learning Models

The App uses two Core ML models that run entirely on your device:

  • Custom YOLOv8 Model - Trained for poker chip detection (4 colors)
  • YOLOv8n Model - General object detection (80 classes)

Both models are embedded in the App and process images locally. No images are sent to external AI services.
